Leaflet Map

New York Dispensary

I had a project to create a leaflet map of dispensary status in New York. Two specific requirements was a plain background (no basemap) and the ability to show the status when the mouse hovers over the counties. This leaflet map uses a feature service that's hosted on ArcGIS Online.
