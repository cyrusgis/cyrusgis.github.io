About me

Hi! My name is Cyrus, and I am a GIS professional based in Vancouver, British Columbia. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Geography from Simon Fraser University and an Advanced Diploma in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) from the British Columbia Institute of Technology. With over a decade of experience in the GIS field, I specialize in spatial data analysis, mapping, and the development of geospatial solutions using Esri technologies. My work focuses on transforming complex spatial datasets into clear, actionable insights through effective visualization and analysis. In addition to GIS software, I have experience working with programming and web technologies including Python, JavaScript, and HTML, which I use to automate workflows, customize GIS applications, and build interactive mapping solutions. I am passionate about using GIS to support data-driven decision-making, whether it involves asset management, spatial modeling, or building interactive web maps and applications. This portfolio showcases a selection of projects that highlight my technical skills, problem-solving approach, and experience working with real-world spatial data.
